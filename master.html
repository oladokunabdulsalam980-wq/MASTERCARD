How to Play

1

Start the Game

Click the "Play Now" button to begin your memory challenge. The cards will be shuffled randomly.

2

Flip Cards

Click on any card to flip it over and reveal its symbol. Remember what you see!

3

Find Matches

Flip another card to find its matching pair. If they match, they'll stay face up. If not, they'll flip back.

4

Complete the Board

Continue flipping cards and finding matches until all pairs are revealed. Try to complete the game with the fewest moves!
